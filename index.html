About Me

Who am I?

I’m Shiva Velishoju, Founder of Velino IDE and an Embedded Systems & Software Developer passionate about building next-generation hardware and intelligent digital ecosystems.

My work combines embedded engineering, IoT, automation, PCB design, and modern software development to create practical technologies that bridge the gap between hardware innovation and user-centric experiences.

I specialize in developing smart systems using ESP32, Arduino, and custom electronics, alongside software platforms built with Electron.js, Node.js, and modern mobile application technologies.

As the creator of projects like Velino IDE, Velino Board, Velino AirPad, and VADDI Calculator, my vision is to build futuristic technologies that empower developers, makers, and everyday users through seamless hardware-software integration and innovative digital experiences.
