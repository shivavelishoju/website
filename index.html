Who am I?

I’m Shiva Velishoju, Founder of Velino IDE and Developer passionate about building Embedded Systems & Software Developer focused on building next-generation hardware and intelligent digital ecosystems. My work combines embedded engineering, IoT, automation, PCB design, and modern software development to create practical technologies that bridge the gap between hardware innovation and user-centric experiences. I specialize in developing smart systems using ESP32, Arduino, and custom electronics, alongside software platforms built with technologies such as Electron.js, Node.js, and mobile application frameworks. As the creator of projects like Velino IDE, Velino Board, Velino AirPad, and VADDI Calculator, I aim to design products that are not only technically advanced but also intuitive, scalable, and impactful in real-world use cases. My vision with Velino is to build a futuristic technology ecosystem that empowers developers, makers, and everyday users through intelligent tools, seamless hardware integration, and innovative digital experiences. From smart automation systems and custom IDE development to interactive applications and embedded product design, I am driven by the passion to transform ambitious ideas into meaningful technology solutions. I believe technology should not only function efficiently but also inspire creativity, simplify complexity, and improve the way people interact with the digital world.
